# Conflict of interest

The author contributed the Mozilla Common Voice Pashto corpus (CV24) and trained the `ihanif/pashto-asr-v3` model used for intelligibility evaluation. The author created the Microsoft Edge TTS Pashto training dataset (`ihanif/pashto-tts-dataset-edge`). No commercial relationship exists with any TTS provider evaluated in this paper. All evaluation code and results are publicly available; the protocol applies identically to all systems regardless of authorship.
