Resources are openly accessible on [Website](https://argyrotsipi.github.io/ParliaBench/) and [GitHub](https://github.com/ArgyroTsipi/ParliaBench).

# Limitations

Our evaluation measures linguistic quality and political authenticity but does not assess argument structure or verify factual accuracy against parliamentary records. Our methods rely entirely on automated metrics without human validation, and LLM-as-a-Judge approaches may carry inherent biases. This work is intended for research and educational purposes, not deployment in actual democratic processes.

# Ethics

Our work establishes a benchmark resource for evaluating LLM-generated parliamentary speech in research and educational contexts. These resources should only be used as assistance to human experts with consideration of their limitations and biases. The parliamentary data is derived from publicly available UK parliamentary proceedings (ParlaMint corpus) licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International, which our derived datasets maintain. Generated parliamentary speeches must be clearly identified as AI-generated content and not misrepresented as authentic political speeches from actual parliamentarians.
